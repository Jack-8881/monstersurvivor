Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure game!

Game Features

  • • Intense survival gameplay
  • • Multiple character choices
  • • Unique power-ups and abilities
  • • Progressive difficulty system

How to Play

  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • • Collect experience points to level up
  • • Choose upgrades wisely
  • • Survive as long as possible

Game Description

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing endless waves of monsters. Level up your character, unlock powerful abilities, and test your skills against increasingly challenging enemies. With its roguelike elements and permanent progression system, every run is a unique experience that will keep you coming back for more.
