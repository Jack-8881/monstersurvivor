Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

Game Features

  • • Endless survival gameplay
  • • Multiple character choices
  • • Unique power-ups and abilities
  • • Progressive difficulty system

How to Play

  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move
  • • Collect experience to level up
  • • Choose upgrades wisely
  • • Survive as long as possible

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival action game where players must face waves of increasingly difficult monsters. With its roguelike elements and permanent progression system, each run offers a unique experience. Choose your character, collect power-ups, and see how long you can survive in this challenging adventure.
