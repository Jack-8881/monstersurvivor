  • • Use WASD or arrow keys to move

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where players face waves of increasingly challenging monsters. With its roguelike elements and strategic gameplay, every session offers a unique experience. Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and see how long you can survive in this addictive game.
