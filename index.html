Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must fight against hordes of monsters. Test your skills and strategy as you progress through increasingly challenging waves of enemies.

Key Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • Multiple character abilities
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Dynamic monster AI
  • Engaging power-ups and upgrades

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Avoid monsters while collecting power-ups and experience points to become stronger. Survive as long as possible and aim for the highest score!
